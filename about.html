About

A director, not just a designer.

I'm Frédéric Oluwasola Idowu — a Creative Director and Multimedia Strategist based in Lagos, working worldwide. For seven years I've taken ambitious mandates — continental campaigns, national programmes, founder pitches — and turned them into work that ministers approve, founders raise on, and audiences remember.

I direct the whole arc: strategy, identity, film, and the team that ships it.

My range is unusual on purpose. I came up as a brand and graphic designer — logos, style guides, the craft — then grew into social strategy that pushed one community past 778,000 followers, and into production: directing crews, drones, live coverage and post.

That combination is why institutions trust me with high-stakes moments. I led continental branding for the African Union / GIZ Afri'Talent Bootcamp, built the identity for Nigeria's first federally funded art residency with the Federal Ministry of Arts, and ran multi-year brand and social work for clients in Houston and Maryland.

I'm bilingual in English and French — with Yoruba, Dioula, Pidgin and working Spanish — which lets me move across markets and cultures without anything getting lost. Calm under deadline, obsessive about craft, and happiest when the numbers move.
